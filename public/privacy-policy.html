Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2025

Summary: BattleGuess is a browser-based game that respects your privacy. We collect minimal data and do not sell your personal information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to BattleGuess ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our web application at www.battleguess.app (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

Local Storage Data: BattleGuess stores game-related data (such as your score and game progress) in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Automatically Collected Information: When you visit our website, our hosting provider may automatically collect standard web server logs, which can include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

3. Third-Party Services

BattleGuess uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Generative AI (Gemini): We use Google's Gemini API to generate images for the game. When images are generated, requests are sent to Google's servers. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data.
  • Google Fonts: We load fonts from Google Fonts, which may result in your browser making requests to Google's servers. See Google Fonts Privacy for details.

4. How We Use Your Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the game experience
  • Improve the Service
  • Monitor usage patterns to ensure the Service functions correctly

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with any third parties except as described in the Third-Party Services section above.

6. Data Retention

Game data stored in your browser's local storage persists until you clear your browser data. Server logs, if collected by our hosting provider, are retained according to their standard retention policies.

7. Cookies

BattleGuess does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. Local storage is used only for game functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

BattleGuess is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited information associated with the Service. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Rights

You can clear all locally stored game data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or site data for battleguess.app. Since we store minimal data on our servers, there is generally no personal data to request deletion of.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
